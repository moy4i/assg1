Introduction

ASIAN KUNG-FU GENERATION is a Japanese alternative rock band formed in Yokohama in 1996, with vocalist Masafumi Gotoh (後藤正文), guitarist Kensuke Kita (喜多建介), Takahiro Yamada (山田貴洋), and drummer Kiyoshi Ijichi (伊地知潔).
