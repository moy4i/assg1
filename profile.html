ASIAN KUNG-FU GENERATION

Formed in 1996 in a university music circle. With Gotoh, Kita, and Yamada being the first ones to create their first-ever band, ASIAN KUNG-FU GENERATION. Ijichi, who was active as a drummer in the same circle, joined. Live performances were performed mainly on campus and in their hometown, Yokohama, with original songs written entirely in English.
